KArSL Statistics

KArSL database consists of 502 sign words constituting the sign words of eleven chapters of ArSL dictionary (Letters, Numbers, Health, Common verbs, Family, Characteristics, Directions and places, Social relationships, In house, Religion, and Jobs and professions).

Each sign of the database is performed by three professional signers. The signers involved in this database are all male and their age is between 30 and 40 years. Each signer repeated each sign 50 times which resulted in a total of 75,300 samples of the whole database (502 x 3 x 50).

Setup and Recording

All signs of KArSL are recorded in an unconstrained environment. We used fixed background (green) to facilitate background removal for researchers who prefer using color video recording. In addition, the signers were not restricted to wear specific clothes or remove eye glasses or watches. Each sign is recorded by each signer in two sessions where the signer wearing different clothes in each session. To add more variety to the database, some signs, alphabets, are performed alternately between the left and right hands of the signer.
