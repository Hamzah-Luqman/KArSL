KArSL Dataset

KArSL is the largest video dataset for Word-Level Arabic sign language (ArSL). The database consists of 502 isolated sign words collected using Microsoft Kinect V2. Each sign of the database is performed by three professional signers. Each signer repeated each sign 50 times which resulted in a total of 75,300 samples of the whole database (502 x 3 x 50).

KArSL Statistics

KArSL database consists of 502 sign words constituting the sign words of eleven chapters of ArSL dictionary (Letters, Numbers, Health, Common verbs, Family, Characteristics, Directions and places, Social relationships, In house, Religion, and Jobs and professions).

Each sign of the database is performed by three professional signers. Each signer repeated each sign 50 times which resulted in a total of 75,300 samples of the whole database (502 x 3 x 50).
All signs are available in three modalities: RGB,  depth, and skeleton joint points.

Dataset Download

Three sets of KArSL dataset are available for researchers:

KArSL-100

KArSL-100 dataset consists of 100 dynamic signs of KArSL dataset (from signID 0071 to 0170). Please follow the links below to download it:

  • Colored video frames: Video's frames extracted, cropped, and resized to 256x256x3.
  • Depth frames: Depth video frames
  • Skeleton joint points

To download the raw video files of this dataset, please follow this link.

KArSL-190

KArSL-190 dataset consists of 190 static and dynamic signs of KArSL dataset (from signID 0001 to 0190). Please follow the links below to download it:

  • Colored video frames: Video's frames extracted, cropped, and resized to 256x256x3.
  • Depth frames : Depth video frames
  • Skeleton joint points

To download the raw video files of this dataset, please follow this link.

KArSL-502

KArSL-502 dataset consists of 502 static and dynamic signs (whole KArSL dataset signs) (from signID 0001 to 0502). Please follow the links below to download it:

  • Colored video frames: Video's frames extracted, cropped, and resized to 256x256x3.
  • Depth frames : Depth video frames
  • Skeleton joint points

To download the raw video files of this dataset, please follow this link.
