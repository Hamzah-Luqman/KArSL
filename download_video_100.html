KArSL Dataset

KArSL is the largest video dataset for Word-Level Arabic sign language (ArSL). The database consists of 502 isolated sign words collected using Microsoft Kinect V2. Each sign of the database is performed by three professional signers. Each signer repeated each sign 50 times which resulted in a total of 75,300 samples of the whole database (502 x 3 x 50).
